How to Register for Local Hack Day: Build

~ A simple guide for TotallyNotRyan ~


1. Head over to Local Hack Day: Build! website
2. Click the "Register" button at the top of the page

*You should now be on the MLH website, specifically the Local Hack Day: Build! event page

3. Click the "Register" button below the time and location details at the top of the page
4. Read the corresponding information and check all the boxes to agree to the MLH and LHD: Build guidelines
5. Login or create an account
You are now Registered! :)

*At the time of the event, make sure to find your way back to the LHD: Build site and check in!
Then, head to the stream on twitch and have fun! :)
